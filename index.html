Monkey Thumb AI

The opposable thumb is a key evolutionary adaptation that allowed primates to manipulate tools and their environment. This dexterity played a crucial role in the development of human intelligence. When considering AI, the monkey's thumb serves as a reminder that even seemingly small biological features can have a profound impact on cognitive abilities. AI researchers can draw inspiration from evolution, seeking to replicate or even surpass the capabilities enabled by the opposable thumb in AI systems.
